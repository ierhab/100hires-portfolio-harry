## Why I chose this source
Justin Welsh built a $10M+ one-person business from scratch after leaving 
corporate. He's not a theorist — he actually did it. This video covers the 
3 core pillars of a sustainable business (pain, attention, offer) which maps 
directly to B2B SaaS growth strategy. His "bleeding neck problem" framework 
is one of the most practical customer discovery tools available.

## Key Insights
- Find the "bleeding neck" problem — pain so deep customers need it solved NOW
- 3 pillars of real business: pain, attention, unique offer
- Quit smart: build runway, pipeline, relationships before leaving
- Use AI (Claude) to extract customer language from interviews
- Long game formula: goals (decades), strategy (yearly), tactics (quarterly)
