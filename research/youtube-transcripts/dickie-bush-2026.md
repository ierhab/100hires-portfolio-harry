## Why I chose this source
Dickie Bush built Ship 30 for 30 into a $5M+ business through systematic 
content and product frameworks. This video reveals his actual goal-setting 
system — not theory, but the exact math-based process he uses to grow 
a digital business. The "bottleneck" framework is directly applicable 
to B2B SaaS growth strategy where identifying the right constraint 
(volume vs efficiency) determines everything.

## Key Insights
- Goals are math — every business is an equation, find the broken variable
- Volume vs efficiency bottleneck — are you missing leads or conversions?
- "More, Better, New" framework — exhaust existing before chasing new
- Impact vs Effort scoring — rank initiatives 1-5 before executing
- Attack quick wins first to build momentum before strategic initiatives
