## I. Executive Insights (Strategic Synthesis)

### 1. Goals are Just Math
The gap between where you are and where you want to be is just an equation. You must identify if your business has a **Volume bottleneck** (not enough leads) or an **Efficiency bottleneck** (leads aren't converting). Scaling is simply finding the part of the equation that needs fixing.

### 2. The "More-Better-New" Framework
Solve bottlenecks in this specific order:
* **More:** Do more of what’s already working.
* **Better:** Improve the quality of current processes.
* **New:** Only start something new if "More" and "Better" are maxed out.

### 3. Impact vs. Effort Ratio
Rank every task on a scale of 1-5 for Impact and Effort. Focus only on high-ratio initiatives (High Impact, Low Effort).
