## Key Insights
- 5 evaluation pillars: capital, complexity, time to profit, management, consistency
- Forex trading fails — high complexity, high capital loss risk
- Drop shipping fails — "hamster wheel" of ads, support, and thin margins
- Real estate excluded — too capital intensive for one-person model
- YouTube automation fails — not lazy at all, needs full team
- Winner: AI Shadow Operating — partner with micro-creators who already 
  have audiences, use AI tools for 90% of execution
- Key insight: use existing assets (other people's audiences) instead of 
  building from zero
- Stack partnerships over time = compounding passive income
