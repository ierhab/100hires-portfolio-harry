For B2B SaaS specifically, HubSpot's findings on AI content saturation and 
brand clarity are directly actionable for 2026 go-to-market strategy

## Key Insights
- AI content oversaturation is real — brand POV and human editing now differentiate
- 5-second test: if a stranger can't understand what you do instantly, reposition
- Stop asking "what should we post?" — ask "why are we posting?"
- Delete content that doesn't teach, entertain, or challenge a belief
- Short-form video = discovery, long-form/podcasts/newsletters = trust
- Use short-form as on-ramp to owned deeper channels
- Traditional website traffic declining as AI answers direct results
- Design website for decision phase, not just top-of-funnel
- Shift from monthly reporting to weekly/daily feedback cycles
- Speed of iteration beats quality of planning in 2026
