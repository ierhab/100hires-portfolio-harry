## Key Insights
- Every entrepreneur hits a "pain line" at $300K, $900K, $2.7M revenue milestones
- Buyback Principle: don't hire to grow, hire to buy back your time
- Your calendar is your capacity constraint — free it up to develop new skills
- Replacement Ladder sequence: Admin → Delivery → Marketing → Sales
- Start with Executive Assistant — highest leverage for lowest cost
- Shift from transactional management to transformational leadership
- Delegate outcomes not tasks — define the result, not the method
- 1-3-1 Rule: 1 problem, 3 options, 1 recommendation — stops you being bottleneck
- Goal: build an "Empire" — unlimited creation you never retire from
