## Post 1: The 4-Step $100k Idea Framework
**Core Concept:** "Niche down 3 levels deep."
1. **Niche Down:** Industry → Niche → Niche-within-a-niche (e.g., Healthcare → PT → PTs on LinkedIn).
2. **Problem List:** Identify 10 biggest pains you've personally experienced.
3. **Desirable Outcomes:** Flip problems into "I" statements (Outcomes).
4. **Single Focus:** Pick the most painful problem and build one product for it.

## Post 2: Sell First, Build Second
**Key Insight:** Don't rush into building based on the first idea.
* Dickie's first product made only $400 because it didn't solve a real problem.
* Scale comes from solving a specific, validated pain point, not just "having an idea."

## Post 3: The "Offer Stacking" Concept
**Key Insight:** Layering value to prevent "Flat Offers."
* Layer value so that the buyer's desire increases as they scroll.
* Use AI to build the offer stack (Product outlines, launch strategies, sales copy).
