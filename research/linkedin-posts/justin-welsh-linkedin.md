## Post 1 — 10 hours ago
I always thought bold people were just built differently than I am. 
I don't believe that anymore.

Back in 2021, my friend Steve made one of the boldest moves I've seen. 
He emailed 50+ investors he'd just raised millions from, told them he was 
shutting the fund down, and returned the money.

But it turns out, Steve had the same fears you or I would have. He'd just 
quietly done the prep work for years.
- He ran tiny experiments outside of VC.
- He had proof he'd be okay financially.
- He hit send while feeling "scared shitless."

We envy the final "bold" move but miss the fear, the prep, and the runway 
people have built to make it all possible.
