# 100Hires Research Project — Harry Zustiman

## Topic Chosen

**LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS**

## Why This Topic

LinkedIn organic is the highest-leverage, lowest-cost growth channel for B2B SaaS in 2026. Decision-makers are already on the platform, organic reach is still meaningful (unlike most social media), and the feedback loop between content and pipeline is faster than SEO or paid ads.

I chose experts who actively practice this founders and creators who have personally built audiences and revenue through LinkedIn, not just consultants who write about it.

## What I Found

The strongest signal across all sources: **distribution consistency beats content volume in 2026.**

Three patterns appeared across almost every expert:

1. **Repurposing beats creating from scratch.** Every high-output creator (Justin Welsh, Ross Simmonds, Dickie Bush) has a system for turning one idea into multiple formats not a new idea every day.

2. **Specificity drives engagement on LinkedIn.** Vague "thought leadership" posts underperform. Posts with a concrete claim, a real number, or a counterintuitive take consistently outperform generic advice.

3. **AI is changing the distribution layer, not the content layer.** The insight still has to be human. AI helps with repurposing, scheduling, and format — not with having something worth saying.
